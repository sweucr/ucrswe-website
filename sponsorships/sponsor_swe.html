Interested in sponsoring?

SWE at UCR has many individual events that are open to sponsorships. We rely mainly on sponsorships to fund our events. Our SWE chapter greatly appreciates all of our sponsors as we wouldn't be able to create events without them.


If you are interested in sponsoring one of our events, or being a main sponsor for SWE at UCR. Contact us at swe.ucr@gmail.com.

Bourns Engineering Day

Bourns Engineering Day is UCR's Bourns College of Engineering's largest outreach event.

Date: April 17th, 2021

If you have any questions, please email sweoutreach.ucr@gmail.com.
Evening With Industry

Evening with Industry (EWI) is a large networking event meant to advance women in engineering by exposing them to industry professionals, resources and opportunities.

Date: October 23, 2020

Engineering R'Future

Engineering R'Future (ERF) is an event dedicated towards high school women to give them a better perspective on the different engineering majors that UCR offers.

Date: TBD, Winter 2021

If you have any questions, please email sweoutreach.ucr@gmail.com.
Research Networking Night

Research Networking Night (RNN) is a joint event held by the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) and American Institute of Chemical Engineers(AiChE) to bring faculty, professors, graduate students and undergraduate students from sciences and engineering together.

Date: November 21th, 2019

If you have any questions, please email swe.ucr@gmail.com.
Rose Hack

Rose Hack is UCR's first women-centric hackathon, hosted by Society of Women Engineers (SWE) the Association for Computing Machinery - Women's Chapter (ACM-W). Rose Hack's mission is to empower women and underrepresented groups in engineering.

Date: January 16-17, 2021

If you have any questions, please email rosehackucr@gmail.com.
