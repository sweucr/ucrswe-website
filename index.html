Aspire. Advance. Achieve.

Welcome! SWE at UCR. SWE is a non-profit, educational, service organization that is driven by members who are passionate about supporting women to achieve full potential in careers as engineers and leaders. We are an active club that hosts academic and professional events through out the year.


We specialize in professional and academic development, increasing female presence in BCOE, and outreach oppnortunities encouraging young students to pursue STEM fields. We also frequently collaborate with other engineering organizations to help expand students' professional network.


Fun Fact! SWE at UCR was founded on November 4th, 1996, making our organization 23 years old!

SWE Linkedin Workshop
Don't know how to make a LinkedIn? Always wondering how to make your LinkedIn better? Say no more! Come out to our workshop where we teach you how to create an effective LinkedIn. As we are approaching recruiting season for internships, it's important to have a great LinkedIn for employers to view and to see what you have accomplished. We'll also have a Neal Goyal, our LinkedIn Campus Ambassador, speak about the importance of LinkedIn and how to use it effectively!

SWE Research Networking Night
Research Networking Night is an opportunity for undergraduates to meet with professors who are looking for undergraduate research. It is a chance to network and discover areas of research that may be of interest and hopefully land a position in a lab. Dinner will be provided.

Rose Hack
Rose Hack is UC Riverside’s women-centric hackathon, founded by the female leaders of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM-W) and the Society of Women Engineers (SWE). Rose Hack’s mission is to encourage individuals from underrepresented groups in STEM to create a strong network together through their shared passions in Science and Technology. Come for a lively weekend of innovation, face masks, discovering and creating new technology, networking, mentorship, and much more.
