Mentorship

The Mentorship Program is a great opportunity to get involved on campus, learn how to succeed academically/professionally, and meet new people.


If you are interested, fill out the application here!


Our Mentorship Program connects undergraduate students, especially new freshman and transfer students, with a guiding mentor for their college career. Through a mentor-mentee relationship, mentors can share their experience and knowledge with their mentees to help them make the most out of their college experience.
